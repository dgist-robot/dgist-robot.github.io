Graduate Programs

To apply for graduate positions (M.S. or Integrated Ph.D.), please contact Prof. Kyungseo Park with the following documents:

  1. Personal Statement (motivation, research interests, plan, etc.)
  2. Current Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  3. Official Academic Transcripts
  4. Portfolio (if available)

Undergraduate Internship

Summer and winter internship positions are available. Interested undergraduates should first check the DGIST RME Department website, then reach out to the professor for additional details about the application procedure.
